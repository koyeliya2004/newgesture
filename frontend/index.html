Features

Everything you need
to create hands-free art

From skeleton tracking to particle effects — every detail is crafted for the best drawing experience.

🖐️

AI Hand Tracking

MediaPipe detects 21 hand landmarks in real-time at 60fps, enabling precise gesture recognition for both hands simultaneously.

✨

Particle Effects

Every stroke spawns glowing particles that follow your fingertip, creating a magical drawing experience with physics-based motion.

🎨

4 Brush Styles

Choose from Round, Square, Spray, and Calligraphy brushes. Adjust size and opacity in real-time for full artistic control.

💀

Skeleton Overlay

See your hand skeleton rendered in real-time on screen — colored by gesture mode so you always know what action is active.

💾

Save Sessions

Save your drawing sessions to the cloud backend. Access your history anytime and download any session as a PNG image.

🌈

4 Canvas Themes

Switch between Dark, Light, Grid, and Chalkboard backgrounds. Each theme transforms the whole visual mood of your artwork.

How it Works

Six gestures.
Infinite expression.

Just show your hand to the camera. The AI does the rest.

1

Open the Studio

Click Launch Studio and allow camera access when prompted. Works best on Chrome or Edge.

2

Show your hand

MediaPipe instantly detects your hand and draws a skeleton overlay — you'll see the gesture mode update in real-time.

3

Use gestures to create

Point your index finger to draw, open palm to erase, pinch to move strokes, peace sign to cycle colors, and more.

4

Save your masterpiece

Save as PNG or save your full session to the cloud. View all your saved works in the History gallery.
