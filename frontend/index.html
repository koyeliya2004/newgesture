Capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Six intuitive gestures give you complete creative control — draw, erase, move, color, undo, and pause — all without touching a thing.

🖐️

Instant Hand Detection

Position your hand toward the camera and the skeleton locks in under a second — tracking 21 landmarks per hand with sub-pixel precision.

✨

Living Particle Strokes

Every line you draw is alive. Particles trail your fingertip in real time, producing fluid, luminous strokes that respond to your speed and motion.

🎨

Four Brush Personalities

Round for sketching, Square for structure, Spray for texture, Calligraphy for expression — switch between them instantly with a gesture or a tap.

💀

Live Skeleton Overlay

Your hand skeleton renders directly onto the canvas — color-coded by active gesture so you always know exactly which mode is engaged.

💾

Personal Session Gallery

Every session is automatically saved. Revisit past work, download any piece as a full-resolution PNG, or continue a session from any device.

🌈

Four Canvas Environments

Dark, Light, Grid, Chalkboard — each theme shifts the entire atmosphere of your workspace. One switch changes everything.

How It Works

Open. Raise. Create.

From browser to first brushstroke in under ten seconds. No download, no install, no configuration required.

1

Open the Studio

Click Launch Studio in Chrome or Edge. One tap — no app to install, no account needed to begin.

2

Allow Camera Access

Grant permission when prompted. AirDraw processes everything locally on your device — your video stream never leaves your browser.

3

Learn Your Gestures

Six gestures cover the entire toolkit: draw, erase, move, color-cycle, undo, and pause. Most people are fluent in under two minutes.

4

Save & Share

Export your artwork as a full-resolution PNG or save the session to your personal gallery — accessible from any device, any time.
